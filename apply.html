How selection works

Three steps, and you'll always know where you stand.

No black box. Every team that applies hears back — here's exactly how the process runs.

1

Apply

You complete the application below — your idea, your incentive mechanism, your team and progress. It takes about 15 minutes.

→ Outcome: a confirmation, and your application enters review.
2

Assessment

SN20's selection team reviews every application. You'll be told either that you weren't selected this round, or that you're moving forward to an interview.

→ Outcome: a clear yes-to-interview, or an honest no.
3

Interview

A call with the selection team — a real discussion about your project. After it, you either take a slot in the intake, or join the waitlist for the next opening.

→ Outcome: a slot, or a place on the waitlist.

Your idea stays yours.

Every application is treated as confidential. Submissions are seen only by SN20's selection team for the purpose of review — they are not shared, published, or used to build competing work. Your idea is safe here.
