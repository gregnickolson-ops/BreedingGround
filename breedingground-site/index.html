Find your place

There are two ways to stand in the Breeding Ground.

SN20 is built for two kinds of people — the teams who want to launch a subnet, and the people who want to grow with the ones who do. Pick your path.

I'm building a subnet

The best on-ramp into Bittensor.

Registering a subnet costs more than most teams can raise alone — and that's before infrastructure, validation, and being noticed. SN20 is the alternative: the support, the rails, and the launch, without surrendering your idea or your upside.

  • A 6-month program — advisory, dev support, marketing, and the funding path to your own subnet.
  • You don't front the registration — a crowdloan funds your launch at graduation.
  • Infrastructure that already exists — run on proven, maintained, bare-metal validation.
  • You keep your subnet — graduate into one that's yours, not someone else's.
  • Your idea stays private — submissions are confidential from the first step.

I want to back the flywheel

One position. Every graduate.

You don't have to pick winners. The SN20 flywheel is engineered so holders, backers and the network all compound together — back it once, and grow with everything it graduates.

  • Hold SN20 — and receive airdrops from every subnet it graduates.
  • Back a specific team — lock TAO into its crowdloan and hold a permanent share of the subnet's emissions once it launches.
  • Built-in alignment — every party compounds at the same time, by design.
  • Be early — to something built to compound for years, not to pump.
